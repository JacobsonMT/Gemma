Data sources

We are indebted to the many researchers who have made data publicly available. A list of published papers that relate to the data included in gemma ishere. If your data is in Gemma, and your paper is not listed, pleaselet us know.

Gemma obtains most data from theGene Expression Omnibus with an increasing contribution fromArrayExpress. We are grateful for the curation efforts at those sites, and the technical assistance we've received. We'd like to especially thank Tanya Barrett (NCBI) for answering many questions about GEO.
