Data sources

We are indebted to the many researchers who have made data publicly available. A list of published papers that relate to the data included in gemma is here. If your data is in Gemma, and your paper is not listed, please let us know.

Gemma obtains most data from the Gene Expression Omnibus with an increasing contribution from ArrayExpress. We are grateful for the curation efforts at those sites, and the technical assistance we've received. We'd like to especially thank Tanya Barrett (NCBI) for answering many questions about GEO.
