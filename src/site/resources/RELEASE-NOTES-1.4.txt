Improvements

     Integration of data visualization/query features on a per-dataset level.
     Download of differential and coexpression data on  a per-dataset level.
     Visualizations in coexp give option of heatmap and line plots.
     90% of data sets analyzed for differential expression (where this is possible)
     Integration of Leon French's automatic tagger
     Cytoscape plugin for coexpression
